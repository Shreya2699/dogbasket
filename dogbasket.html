About

DogBasket is a leading social media pet app connecting dog owners in a community where they share their dogs’ daily lives, from the dogs' perspective. The dog perspective fulfills a special mission to break down barriers between people in a unique way. This is because when people speak as their snuggle buddies, they tend to be really nice and loving to each other. We are truly the “cutest community on the planet.”
